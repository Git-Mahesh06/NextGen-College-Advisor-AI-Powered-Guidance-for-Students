### NextGen-College-Advisor-AI-Powered-Guidance-for-Students
- Applying to colleges often requires students to visit multiple college websites to gather admission criteria, making the process time-consuming and challenging. 
- Using Linear Regression method, our system predicts a student's chances of acceptance into various colleges based on their ranks and branch-specific cutoffs.
- This platform streamlines the application process, allowing students to make informed decisions and apply to colleges where they have a higher likelihood of acceptance.
